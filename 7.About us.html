About us

Dream Cliff Mountain Resort, a family-owned luxury travel business founded by Farzaan Jabir, Sarah Nawaz, and Naushard Jabir, is located in Sri Lanka. The resort, built in memory of Adam and Eve, offers a unique experience in the tranquility of nature and the splendor of the surrounding mountain ranges. The resort was built to provide clients with the best of the world, capturing the essence of nature and the splendor of the surrounding mountain ranges.
