Indoor Events

Play area

For the little ones on vacation, there is a joyful and playful refuge. Both an indoor and an outdoor playground are available for children at our hotel. For kids under five, there is an indoor play area that is supervised by a child care assistant who is trained to treat youngsters with love and care. The perfect place for kids to play while parents can have some "We time" is in a space with colorful play equipment.

Board & card games

Playing board games with your friends and family is one of the easier but still extremely enjoyable things you can do inside your hotel. There are many various board games you may play with your friends and family. From classics like Monopoly, Scrabble, and Snakes and Ladders to intriguing ones like Risk and Game of Life, there are many different board games to choose from. Along with your standard variety of board games, playing card games indoors during the rainy season is also a terrific option and will undoubtedly be entertaining for you. While you wait for the rain to stop, playing a game of UNO or Monopoly Deal will be a lot of fun.

Functions

As an indoor event held in connection with our hotel, various functions can be shown. You will get the support of our staff to carry out those functions as you wish. Decorations can also be done as you wish.
