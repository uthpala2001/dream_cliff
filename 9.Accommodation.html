“Our rooms are individual cabanas,

with breathtaking and unobstructed views”

King Beehive & Queen Beehive

Each "Beehive" accommodation is a solitary cabana with a breathtaking, unobstructed view from the bed to the balcony and beyond to the mountains and valleys. All beehives are round, equipped with a dome roof, private balcony or patio, en-suite bathroom, and glass-tempered doors. As you take in the breathtaking views from your bed, the plush bedding and linens make you feel light, cozy, and at home, as though you were up in the sky. The resort includes a one-of-a-kind Infinity 'Tsunami' Swimming Pool where guests can unwind and enjoy the scenery.
