Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service

About our hotel

Dream Cliff Mountain Resort offers lodging with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden, and a patio in Haputale, which is 21 kilometers from Demodara Nine Arch Bridge. There is a restaurant at this five-star hotel. There is room service, free WiFi, an airport shuttle, and a 24-hour front desk at the establishment.


Rooms

Each room in the hotel has a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linens, towels, and a balcony with views of the mountains. Every room has a safety deposit box, and some also provide patios or pool views. All rooms also have access to a safety deposit box. The Dream Cliff Mountain Resort offers complimentary amenities and CD players in each of its guest rooms.
