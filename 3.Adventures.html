Are you ready for an adventure?

Flying Ravana Zipline

A unique experience is provided in Sri Lanka at the Flying Ravana Adventure Park, which is situated in Ella's lush green estates. The first Mega zip-line in Sri Lanka was proudly finished. The two-wire zip-line is almost half a kilometer long, travels at 80 kph, and provides a bird's-eye perspective of the island's stunning highlands.

Air Rifling

A fantastic method to develop a keen eye, a steady hand, and a rapid reaction for anyone who wants to relax in a way that is utterly addictive. aided by a knowledgeable, amiable, and skilled group of professionals who would give you a memorable experience.

Archery

Come experience the thrill of our outdoor archery range with us; it's a special way to spend time with loved ones and friends while also picking up a new skill. Visitors to Flying Ravana, Ella, may take part in all the activities and watch from the archery range, which is on the property.
