Our restaurants serve colourful and aromatic Sri Lankan cuisine,

along with a selection of European dishes

Honey Comb Restaurant

Atop a hill, Honey Comb Restaurant commands a prime position for viewing the opening in the mountain range. It has an attractive design, an ethnic rustic decor, and magnificent views from every seat. The eatery serves a variety of European dishes in addition to its specialty Sri Lankan dishes.

Sky Deck Restaurant

upper floor deck, known as Sky Deck, is comparable to the clouds. It provides a full 360-degree view of the mountain ranges around. Up to five Sri Lankan districts, Badulla, Monergala, Ratnapura, Hambanthota, and Ampara, are visible. The restaurant offers breakfast as well as dinner and barbecue in the evening. Any time of day is a good time to enjoy a cup of tea.
