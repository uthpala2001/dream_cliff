Yoga

For people of various body shapes, ages, and levels of expertise, yoga as taught by YFI is an accessible practice. Making sure that nobody encounters obstacles to practicing yoga is a key component of YFI's purpose. We are dedicated to diversity, inclusiveness, flexibility, and accessibility. Yoga, which incorporates vigorous breathing exercises, movement, mantra, and meditation, focuses on the energy aspect of the body.
